Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from Jabalpur business owners

How long will my website take to deliver?

For a basic 4–6 page brochure site we typically deliver within 48 hours after receiving content and approval for the design.

Will you help with images and copy?

Yes — we offer basic copy and image suggestions. For detailed content creation we offer paid add-ons.

Do you provide hosting and SSL?

We offer a 1-year hosting option including SSL. Hosting renewal and maintenance are available as yearly plans.

Can I update my site later?

Yes — for dynamic sites we provide an admin panel. For static sites we can update content on request (paid small updates).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfer and online payments (Stripe/Razorpay) depending on your preference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Short helpful answers for business owners in Jabalpur.

How long will my website be ready?+

Typically 2–5 days for a 4–6 page brochure site after we receive content (text & images).

Is domain registration included?+

Bronze: domain not included. Silver & Gold: we help register and configure; domain cost extra.

How do payments work?+

Payments are typically split into three parts: advance, mid-review, and final. We accept UPI, bank transfer and online payment options.

Can I update the site myself later?+

Yes — Bronze/Silver are static; small changes we can do. Gold can include an admin panel for self-updates.

Do you help with Google Business Profile?+

Yes — basic help with Google Business listing is available as an add-on or included in Silver/Gold depending on plan.

What support do you provide after delivery?+

Minor static fixes ₹200, major static updates ₹500–700, dynamic updates ₹700–1000. We also offer yearly maintenance plans.

Do you create logos and photos?+

Yes — simple logos and stock image suggestions available for a small fee. Full branding quoted separately.

Do I need to visit your office?+

No — most work is remote. Local in-person meetings can be arranged in Vijay Nagar, Jabalpur if needed.

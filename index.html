Frequently Asked Questions

Short answers — clear and useful for local business owners.

How long will my website be ready?+

For a simple brochure site (4–6 pages) we usually finish in 2–5 days after you provide the content (text & images). More custom features take longer and we will share a timeline before starting.

Is domain registration included?+

Bronze plan: domain not included. Silver and Gold: we include help to register and configure the domain (domain cost charged separately if needed).

How do payments work?+

We take payments in three parts: Advance (to start), Mid (on review), Final (on delivery). We accept UPI, bank transfer and online payment options.

Can I update the site later myself?+

Most Bronze/Silver sites are static—small changes we can make quickly. For Gold (dynamic/admin) you can get an admin interface. We also offer paid update support.

Do you help with Google/online listing?+

Yes — basic help with Google Business Profile / local listing is available as an add-on or included as part of Silver/Gold services depending on the package.

What content do you need from me?+

Business name, address (we will use Vijay Nagar, Jabalpur if confirmed), phone/WhatsApp, 3–6 images (we can suggest stock photos), list of services and any logo you have.

Do you create logos or photos?+

Yes — we can create a simple logo and suggest stock images for a small fee. Complex branding is quoted separately.

Do I need to visit your office?+

No — most work is remote. If you prefer an in-person meeting we can arrange one locally in Vijay Nagar, Jabalpur.

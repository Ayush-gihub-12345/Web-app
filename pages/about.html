About WebpageWale

WebpageWale is a modern web agency based in Jabalpur focused on building affordable, mobile-first websites for small businesses, clinics, shops, coaching institutes and freelancers. Our goal is to make high-quality websites accessible without high agency pricing.

We deliver brochure websites in as fast as 2–5 days once content is shared. Our process is simple, transparent and designed for local business owners who want a clean, fast, easy-to-manage website.

We provide support for domain setup, hosting, Google profile, SEO basics and ongoing content updates. For quick updates we offer minor edits (₹200), larger static edits (₹500–700) and dynamic/admin system updates (₹700–1000).

With WebpageWale, you receive reliable service, clear pricing and quick help — all from a local Jabalpur team.
